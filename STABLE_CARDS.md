# Stable Cards

Cards that interact with stables in any way: building stables (cost reduction, free builds, triggers), placing stables as storage, gaining animals/benefits when stables are built, scoring based on stables, or using the "Build Stables" action.

Covers all expansions (Base Revised, Artifex, Bubulcus, Consul Dirigens, Corbarius, Dulcinaria) and all player counts (1+, 3+, 4+).

---

## Minor Improvements

### Stable Cost Reduction / Free Stable Builds

**Mining Hammer** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #16)*
> When you play this card, you immediately get 1 food. Each time you renovate, you can also build a stable without paying wood.

**Shelter** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #1, passing minor)*
> You can immediately build a stable at no cost, but only if you place it in a pasture covering exactly 1 farmyard space.

**Stable** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #2, passing minor)*
> Immediately build 1 stable. (The stable costs you nothing, but you must pay the cost shown on this card.)

**Feed Fence** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #56)*
> For each new stable you build, you get 1 food—for your last one, get 3 food. Each time you build stables, you can build exactly 1 stable for 1 clay instead of wood.

**Wooden Whey Bucket** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #16)*
> Each time before you use the 'Sheep Market'/'Cattle Market' accumulation space, you can build exactly 1 stable for 1 wood/at no cost.

### Stable Build Triggers (Resources/Food on Build)

**Farmyard Manure** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #43)*
> Each time you build 1 or more stables in one turn, you place 1 food on each of the next 3 round spaces. At the start of these rounds, you get the food.

**Stable Tree** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #74)*
> Each time you build 1 or more stables on your turn, place 1 wood on each of the next 3 round spaces. At the start of these rounds, you get the wood.

**Carpenter's Axe** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #15)*
> Each time after you use a wood accumulation space, if you then have at least 7 wood in your supply, you can build exactly 1 stable for 1 wood.

**Toolbox** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #27)*
> In the work phase, after each turn in which you build at least 1 room, stable, or fence, you can build the 'Joinery', 'Pottery', or 'Basketmaker's Workshop' major improvement.

---

### Stable Scoring

**Muck Rake** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #29)*
> During scoring, you get 1 bonus point for exactly 1 unfenced stable holding exactly 1 sheep. The same applies to wild boar and cattle, if held in different unfenced stables.

---

### Stables as Animal Storage / Capacity

**Market Stall** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #54)*
> Cost: 1 Stable from Your Supply. After the field phase of each harvest, you can exchange 1 grain plus 1 fence (both from your supply) for 5 food.

**Beer Stall** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #49)*
> In the feeding phase of each harvest, for each empty unfenced stable you have, you can exchange 1 grain for 5 food.

**Tumbrel** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #54)*
> When you play this card, you immediately get 2 food. Each time after you take an unconditional 'Sow' action, you get 1 food for each stable you have.

**Stable Yard** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #50)*
> Prerequisites: 3 Stables and 3 Pastures. When you play this card, you immediately get 1 food for each completed round left to play. At any time, you can exchange 1 sheep plus 1 wild boar for 1 cattle.

**Stable Manure** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #72)*
> Prerequisites: At Most 1 Occupation. In the field phase of each harvest, you can harvest 1 additional good from a number of fields equal to the number of unfenced stables you have.

## Occupations

### Stable Cost Reduction / Free Stable Builds

**Groom** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #89, Players: 1+)*
> When you play this card, you immediately get 1 wood. Once you live in a stone house, at the start of each round, you can build exactly 1 stable for 1 wood.

**Carpenter's Apprentice** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #88, Players: 1+)*
> Wood rooms cost you 2 woods less. Your 3rd and 4th stable each cost you 1 wood less. Your 13th to 15th fence each cost you nothing.

**Stablehand** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #89, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you build at least 1 fence, you can also build a stable without paying wood for the stable.

**Casual Worker** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #149, Players: 4+)*
> Each time another player uses a 'Quarry' accumulation space, you can choose to get 1 food or build a stable without paying wood.

---

### Stable Build Triggers (Animals/Resources on Build)

**Stockman** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #168, Players: 4+)*
> When you build your 2nd/3rd/4th stable, you immediately get 1 cattle/wild boar/sheep, even if built on the same turn (but not retroactively).

**Stable Milker** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #166, Players: 4+)*
> Each time you build at least 2 stables on the same turn, you also get 1 cattle.

**Breeder Buyer** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #167, Players: 4+)*
> Each time you build at least 1 wood/clay/stone room and at least 1 stable on the same turn, you also get 1 sheep/wild boar/cattle.

**Pasture Master** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #168, Players: 4+)*
> Each time you renovate, you get 2 food and 1 additional animal of the respective type in each of your pastures with stable.

**Millwright** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #88, Players: 1+)*
> You immediately get 1 grain. Each time you build fences, stables, and rooms, or renovate your house, you can replace up to 2 building resources of any type with 1 grain each.

---

### Future / Scheduled Stable Builds

**Stable Planner** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #89, Players: 1+)*
> Add 3, 6, and 9 to the current round. You can place 1 stable on each corresponding round space. At the start of these rounds (not earlier), you can build the stable at no cost.

**Sample Stable Maker** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #102, Players: 1+)*
> At the start of each returning home phase, you can return a built stable to your supply to get 1 wood, 1 grain, 1 food, and a 'Minor Improvement' action.

---

### Stable Scoring

**Stable Architect** *(Base Revised, Deck A, #98, Players: 1+)*
> During scoring, you get 1 bonus point for each unfenced stable in your farmyard.

**Stall Holder** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #101, Players: 1+)*
> Once per round, if you have 0/1/2/3/4 unfenced stables on your farm, you can exchange 2 grain for 1 bonus point and 1/2/3/4/5 food.

**Animal Activist** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #136, Players: 3+)*
> If there are still 3/6/9 complete rounds left to play, you immediately get 2/3/4 wood. During scoring, each player with the most fenced stables gets 2 bonus points.

**Lord of the Manor** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #100, Players: 1+)*
> During scoring, you get 1 bonus point for each scoring category in which you score the maximum 4 points. (The bonus point is also awarded for 4 fenced stables.)

---

### "Build Stables" Action Access

**Stable Cleaner** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #94, Players: 1+)*
> At any time, you can take the 'Build Stables' action without placing a person. If you do, each stable costs you 1 wood and 1 food.

**Stable Master** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #89, Players: 1+)*
> When you play this card, you can immediately build exactly 1 stable for 1 wood. Exactly one of your unfenced stables can hold up to 3 animals of one type.

**Stagehand** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #150, Players: 4+)*
> Each time another player uses the 'Traveling Players' accumulation space, you can take your choice of a 'Build Fences', 'Build Stables', or 'Build Rooms' action.

**Lumber Virtuoso** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #129, Players: 3+)*
> Each harvest in which you have at least 5 wood in your supply, you can discard down to 5 wood to take a 'Build Stables' or 'Build Wood Rooms' action by paying the usual costs.

---

### Stables Used for Non-Standard Purposes

**Farm Hand** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #85, Players: 1+)*
> Once you have 4 field tiles arranged in a 2x2, you can use a 'Build Stables' action to build a stable in the center of the 2x2. This stable provides room for a person but no animal.

**Open Air Farmer** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #149, Players: 4+)*
> When you play this card, you remove exactly 3 stables in your supply from play to build a pasture covering 2 farmyard spaces. You only need to pay a total of 2 wood for fences.
