# Room Cards

Cards that interact with rooms in any way: building rooms (cost reduction, free builds, discounts), gaining benefits/resources when rooms are built, scoring based on rooms, family growth via rooms, or using the "Build Rooms" action.

Covers all expansions (Base Revised, Artifex, Bubulcus, Consul Dirigens, Corbarius, Dulcinaria) and all player counts (1+, 3+, 4+).

---

## Minor Improvements

### Room Cost Reduction

**Carpenter's Parlor** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #13)*
> Wooden rooms only cost you 2 wood and 2 reed each.

**Carpenter's Hammer** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #14)*
> Each time you build at least 2 wood/clay/stone rooms at once, you get a total discount of 2 reed as well as 2 wood/3 clay/4 stone.

**Clay Supports** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #15)*
> Each time you build a clay room, you can pay 2 clay, 1 wood, and 1 reed instead of 5 clay and 2 reed.

**Straw-Thatched Roof** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #14)*
> Prerequisites: 3 Grain Fields. You no longer need reed to renovate or build a room.

**Wood Slide Hammer** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #13)*
> On your first renovation, if you have at least 5 wood rooms, you can renovate to stone directly and you get a discount of 2 stone on the renovation cost.

### Room Building Triggers (Resources/Benefits on Build)

**Baseboards** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #4, passing minor)*
> You immediately get 1 wood for each room you have. If you have more rooms than people, you get 1 additional wood.

**Remodeling** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #5, passing minor)*
> You immediately get 1 clay for each clay room and for each major improvement you have.

**Gift Basket** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #73)*
> When you play this card, if you have exactly 2/3/4/5 rooms, you immediately get 1 vegetable/food/grain/vegetable.

**Toolbox** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #27)*
> In the work phase, after each turn in which you build at least 1 room, stable, or fence, you can build the 'Joinery', 'Pottery', or 'Basketmaker's Workshop' major improvement.

**Hammer Crusher** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #14)*
> Immediately before you renovate to stone, you get 2 clay and 1 reed and you can take a 'Build Rooms' action.

---

### Family Growth via Rooms

**Family Friend Home** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #21)*
> Each time you take a 'Build Rooms' action while having more rooms than people already, you also get a 'Family Growth' action and 1 food.

**Heart of Stone** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #21)*
> Each time a 'Quarry' accumulation space is revealed, if you have room in your house, you can immediately take a 'Family Growth' action without placing a person.

**Bed in the Grain Field** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #24)*
> At the start of the next harvest, you get a 'Family Growth' action if you have room for the newborn.

**Stork's Nest** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #10)*
> Prerequisites: 5 Occupations. In the returning home phase of each round, if you have more rooms than people, you can pay 1 food to take a 'Family Growth' action.

---

### Room Scoring

**Half-Timbered House** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #30)*
> During scoring, you get 1 bonus point for each stone room you have. You can only use one card to get bonus points for your stone house.

**Luxurious Hostel** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #34)*
> During scoring, if you then have more stone rooms than people, you get 4 bonus points. You can only use one card to get bonus points for your stone house.

---

### Rooms Providing Extra Capacity / Special Housing

**Wooden Shed** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #10)*
> This card can only be played via a 'Major Improvement' action. It provides room for one person. You may no longer renovate.

**Bunk Beds** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #10)*
> Prerequisites: 2 Major Improvements. Once you have 4 rooms, your house can hold 5 people.

**Milking Place** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #12)*
> In the feeding phase of each harvest, you get 1 food. You can no longer hold animals in your house (not even via another card).

---

### Room Count Triggers / Synergies

**Renovation Company** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #13)*
> Prerequisites: In Wooden House with Exactly 2 Rooms. When you play this card, you immediately get 3 clay. Immediately after, you can renovate without paying any building resources.

**Hawktower** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #14)*
> Place a stone room on round space 12. If you live in a stone house at the start of the round, you can build the stone room at no cost. Otherwise, discard the stone room.

**Civic Facade** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #48)*
> Prerequisites: 3 Rooms. Before the start of each round, if you have more occupations than improvements in your hand, you get 1 food.

## Occupations

### Room Cost Reduction

**Carpenter** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #126, Players: 1+)*
> Every new room only costs you 3 of the appropriate building resource and 2 reed (e.g. if you live in a wooden house, 3 wood and 2 reed).

**Brushwood Collector** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #145, Players: 3+)*
> Each time you renovate or build a room, you can replace the required 1 or 2 reed with a total of 1 wood.

**Stonecutter** *(Base Revised, Deck A, #143, Players: 3+)*
> Every improvement, room, and renovation costs you 1 stone less.

**Frame Builder** *(Base Revised, Deck A, #123, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you build a room/renovate, but only once per room/action, you can replace exactly 2 clay or 2 stone with 1 wood.

**Bricklayer** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #122, Players: 1+)*
> Each improvement and each renovation cost you 1 clay less. Each room costs you 2 clay less.

**Clay Plasterer** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #121, Players: 1+)*
> Renovating to clay only costs you exactly 1 clay and 1 reed. Each clay room only costs you 3 clay and 2 reed to build.

**Carpenter's Apprentice** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #88, Players: 1+)*
> Wood rooms cost you 2 woods less. Your 3rd and 4th stable each cost you 1 wood less. Your 13th to 15th fence each cost you nothing.

**Wooden Hut Extender** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #128, Players: 3+)*
> Wood rooms now cost you 1 reed, and additionally 5 wood through round 5, 4 wood in rounds 6 and 7, and 3 wood in round 8 and later.

**Renovation Preparer** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #123, Players: 1+)*
> For each new wood/clay room you build, you get 2 clay/2 stone.

### Room Building Triggers (Resources/Benefits on Build)

**Roughcaster** *(Base Revised, Deck A, #110, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you build at least 1 clay room or renovate your house from clay to stone, you also get 3 food.

**Wall Builder** *(Base (Revised), Deck A, #111, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you build at least 1 room, you can place 1 food on each of the next 4 round spaces. At the start of these rounds, you get the food.

**Rustic** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #111, Players: 1+)*
> For each clay room you build, you get 2 food and 1 bonus point. (This does not apply to stone rooms and renovated wood rooms.)

**Furnisher** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #96, Players: 1+)*
> When you play this card, you immediately get 2 wood. After each new room you build, you can build or play 1 improvement for 1 wood less.

**Riparian Builder** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #128, Players: 3+)*
> Each time another player uses the 'Reed Bank' accumulation space, you can build a room: if you build a clay/stone room, you get a discount of 1 clay/2 stone.

**Journeyman Bricklayer** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #163, Players: 4+)*
> When you play this card, you immediately get 2 stone. Each time another player renovates to stone or builds a stone room, you get 1 stone.

**Resource Recycler** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #149, Players: 4+)*
> Each time another player renovates to stone, if you live in a clay house, you can pay 2 food to build a clay room at no additional cost.

**Building Tycoon** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #128, Players: 3+)*
> Each time after another player builds 1 or more rooms, you can give them 1 food to build exactly 1 room yourself. (You must pay the building cost of the room.)

**Breeder Buyer** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #167, Players: 4+)*
> Each time you build at least 1 wood/clay/stone room and at least 1 stable on the same turn, you also get 1 sheep/wild boar/cattle.

**Millwright** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #88, Players: 1+)*
> You immediately get 1 grain. Each time you build fences, stables, and rooms, or renovate your house, you can replace up to 2 building resources of any type with 1 grain each.

### "Build Rooms" Action Access

**Cottager** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #87, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you use the 'Day Laborer' action space, you can also either build exactly 1 room or renovate your house. Either way, you have to pay the cost.

**House Artist** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #149, Players: 4+)*
> Each time you use the 'Traveling Players' accumulation space, you also get a 'Build Rooms' action. Each room you build during the action costs you 1 reed less.

**Stagehand** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #150, Players: 4+)*
> Each time another player uses the 'Traveling Players' accumulation space, you can take your choice of a 'Build Fences', 'Build Stables', or 'Build Rooms' action.

**Wood Barterer** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #119, Players: 1+)*
> Each time before you use an action space with a 'Build Fences' or 'Build Rooms' action, you can choose to either get 2 wood or exchange up to 2 wood for 1 reed each.

**Henpecked Husband** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #94, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you take a 'Build Rooms' action with the second person you place, return the first person you placed home, unless it is on the 'Meeting Place' action space.

**Recreational Carpenter** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #130, Players: 3+)*
> At the end of each work phase in which you did not use the 'Meeting Place' action space, you can take a 'Build Rooms' action without placing a person.

**Hardworking Man** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #127, Players: 3+)*
> This card is an action space for you only. If each other player has more rooms than you, it provides the 'Day Laborer', 'Building Rooms', and 'Major Improvement' actions (all three).

**Lumber Virtuoso** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #129, Players: 3+)*
> Each harvest in which you have at least 5 wood in your supply, you can discard down to 5 wood to take a 'Build Stables' or 'Build Wood Rooms' action by paying the usual costs.

---

### Family Growth via Rooms

**Bed Maker** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #93, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you add rooms to your house, you can also pay 1 wood and 1 grain to immediately get a 'Family Growth with Room Only' action.

**Little Stick Knitter** *(Bubulcus Expansion, Deck B, #92, Players: 1+)*
> From Round 5 on, each time you use the 'Sheep Market' accumulation space, you can also take a 'Family Growth with Room Only' action.

---

### Room Scoring

**House Steward** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #136, Players: 3+)*
> If there are still 1/3/6/9 complete rounds left to play, you immediately get 1/2/3/4 wood. During scoring, each player with the most rooms gets 3 bonus points.

**Butler** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #100, Players: 1+)*
> If you play this card in round 11 or before, during scoring, you get 4 bonus points if you then have more rooms than people.

**Timber Shingle Maker** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #132, Players: 3+)*
> When you renovate to stone, you can place up to 1 wood from your supply in each of your rooms. During scoring, each such wood is worth 1 bonus point.

**Cow Prince** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #134, Players: 3+)*
> During scoring, you get 1 bonus point for each space in your farmyard (including rooms) holding at least 1 cattle.

**Master Bricklayer** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #95, Players: 1+)*
> Each time you build a major improvement, reduce the stone cost by the number of rooms you have built onto your initial house.

### Room Count Triggers / Synergies

**Small-scale Farmer** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #118, Players: 1+)*
> As long as you live in a house with exactly 2 rooms, at the start of each round, you get 1 wood.

**Childless** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #114, Players: 1+)*
> At the start of each round, if you have at least 3 rooms but only 2 people, you get 1 food and 1 crop of your choice (grain or vegetable).

**Roof Ballaster** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #123, Players: 1+)*
> When you play this card, you can immediately pay 1 food to get 1 stone for each room you have.

**Freemason** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #123, Players: 1+)*
> As long as you live in a clay/stone house with exactly 2 rooms, at the start of each work phase, you get 2 clay/stone.

**Priest** *(Base Revised, Deck A, #125, Players: 1+)*
> When you play this card, if you live in a clay house with exactly 2 rooms, you immediately get 3 clay, 2 reed, and 2 stone.

**Pastor** *(Base Revised, Deck B, #163, Players: 4+)*
> Once you are the only player to live in a house with only 2 rooms, you immediately get 3 wood, 2 clay, 1 reed, and 1 stone (only once).

**Domestician Expert** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #148, Players: 4+)*
> You can keep 2 sheep on the border between each pair of orthogonally adjacent rooms.

### Rooms Providing Extra Capacity / Special Housing

**Animal Tamer** *(Base Revised, Deck A, #86, Players: 1+)*
> When you play this card, you immediately get your choice of 1 wood or 1 grain. Instead of just 1 animal total, you can keep any 1 animal in each room of your house.

**Homekeeper** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #85, Players: 1+)*
> Exactly one clay or stone room in your house can hold an additional person if the room is adjacent to both a field and a pasture.

**Lodger** *(Artifex Expansion, Deck A, #127, Players: 3+)*
> This card provides room for one person, but only until the returning home phase of round 9. If, by then, there is no room elsewhere for that person, remove it from play.

**Den Builder** *(Corbarius Expansion, Deck C, #85, Players: 1+)*
> When you live in a clay or stone house, you can pay 1 grain and 2 food. If you do, for the rest of the game, this card provides room for exactly one person.

**Reader** *(Dulcinaria Expansion, Deck D, #85, Players: 1+)*
> As soon as you have 6 occupations in front of you (including this one), this card provides room for one person. In the draft variant, you need 7 occupations to play this.

### Free / Bonus Room Builds

**Mason** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck C, #87, Players: 1+)*
> Place a stone room on this card. Once you have a stone house with at least 4 rooms, at any time, you can add that room without paying any building resources.

**Master Builder** *(Consul Dirigens Expansion, Deck D, #87, Players: 1+)*
> Once your house has at least 5 rooms, at any time, but only once this game, you can add another room at no cost.
